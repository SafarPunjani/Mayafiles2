Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Read how MAYA protects and respects your personal information.

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the MAYA website. MAYA ("we," "our," or "us") is a jewellery brand based in Jamnagar, Gujarat, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or interact with our brand.

By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy. We are committed to safeguarding your personal data and ensuring a secure browsing experience.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any details you voluntarily provide through our contact form.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent to us via WhatsApp, email, or the contact form on our website.
  • Usage Data: Non-personal information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the website, collected through standard web analytics.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience and remember your preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and communicate with you about our jewellery products.
  • To provide customer support and address any questions or concerns.
  • To improve our website experience and ensure content is relevant and useful.
  • To send you updates about new collections or promotions, only if you have opted in to receive such communications.
  • To maintain the security and functionality of our website.

4. Data Protection & Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Third-Party Sharing

MAYA does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with external marketing companies or advertisers.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal proceedings.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of MAYA, our customers, or the public.
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

6. WhatsApp Communication

When you contact us via WhatsApp using the links on our website, your phone number and message content are processed through WhatsApp's platform. We use this information solely to respond to your product enquiries and provide customer service. Please refer to WhatsApp's own privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help us understand how visitors interact with our website. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

  • Product images are for representation purposes. Actual products may vary slightly in colour and finish due to screen settings and photography lighting.
  • Prices displayed on the website are indicative and may change without prior notice. Final pricing will be confirmed during your enquiry.
  • This website is intended for informational purposes only and does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy.
  • We reserve the right to update or modify these terms at any time without prior notice.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt out of any marketing communications at any time.
